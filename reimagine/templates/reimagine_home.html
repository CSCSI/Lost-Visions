Competition Information

General Information

The REimagine competition encourages the creative reimagining of an illustration or illustrations from The Illustration Archive:

Participants are invited to create a work of art or craft inspired by an illustration(s) and to submit their entries online by 14th December 2015.

Entries will be judged by a selected panel of experts from the creative industries and the work of nominated winners will be exhibited in Cardiff University and in an online exhibition.

Prizes will be awarded to winners and runners up in two categories: over and under 16s.

How to enter

  • Log in to The Illustration Archive using the button below

  • Choose an illustration (or illustrations) from the Archive

  • Reimagine the illustration(s). See our YouTube video for some ideas

  • Fill in the entry form and upload your entry

That's it! Enjoy reimagining these illustrations from the past!
